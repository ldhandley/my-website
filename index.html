About Me

Hi, I’m Lindsey, a nature-loving gardener from Kitsap County in the beautiful Pacific Northwest. I've been gardening for three years—still a beginner, but falling more in love with it every season. One of my favorite moments last year was watching hummingbirds flit around my gladiolus blooms—it was magical!

My Gardening Philosophy

I believe that nature is sacred and that everyone can do their part to help restore and protect the native flora and fauna of the PNW. My garden is a small but intentional space where native plants can thrive, and pollinators and birds can find refuge. I never use pesticides—just patience, observation, and a deep respect for the natural world.

How It All Started

Believe it or not, my love of gardening was first sparked by a video game—Harvest Moon—in the 1990s. Years later, that spark turned into a real connection to my home and region through gardening. Creating this garden has helped me feel more rooted in the PNW than ever before.
